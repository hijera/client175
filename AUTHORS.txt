Mutagen
========================================================================
Copyright (C) 2005  Michael Urman

Mutagen is licensed under the GPL version 2 or later license.

Mutagen is a Python module to handle audio metadata. It supports ASF, 
FLAC, M4A, Monkey's Audio, MP3, Musepack, Ogg FLAC, Ogg Speex, Ogg Theora, 
Ogg Vorbis, True Audio, WavPack and OptimFROG audio files. All versions 
of ID3v2 are supported, and all standard ID3v2.4 frames are parsed. It 
can read Xing headers to accurately calculate the bitrate and length of 
MP3s. ID3 and APEv2 tags can be edited regardless of audio format. It 
can also manipulate Ogg streams on an individual packet/page level. 

License:  mutagen/COPYING.txt
Website:  http://code.google.com/p/mutagen/
